## Objective - Establish a secure and financially controlled foundation for Ideal-Studios.

 Before provisioning any infrastructure, a secure foundation is paramount. A 'Security-First' approach ensures that the environment is protected from the root level and that costs are strictly monitored.

 ---

 ### Identity and Access Management (IAM)
 * Root Account securitisation: In order to prevent unauthorised global access on the root account, Multifactor Authentication (MFA) was enabled.
 * Administrative Isolation: A dedicated Ideal-Admin IAM was created following the principle of least privilege. All subsequent architectural structuring was performed using this account.

---

### Financial Governance
* Budget Alarms: To minimise costs, a budget alarm with a threshold of $0.01 was implemented.
* SNS Alerts were set up to notify in case the free tier is exceeded. As the saying goes, "A good cloud Engineer is a also a good Cloud Economoist"
